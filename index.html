Who Am I?

I'm a Software Engineer with a dedicated focus toward Responsive Web Design & Web Development. I have a comprehensive background and experience with the Python & MEAN full stacks, a proven ability to develop skills quickly, and a desire to continue to learn. I'm also a dedicated volunteer with a passion, and a belief in, helping and understanding others.

Technology has always been a passion of mine. As part of the "Oregon Trail Generation", I grew up playing the popular video game and I recall trying to code my own version. The enjoyment of playing that game and the satisfaction of seeing my code actually do what I exepected it to do (e.g., coding my name in BASIC to scroll diagonally across the screen), led me into Software Development. I continue to have that passion and I'm able to express it and find those, "that's cool!" moments, through web design and development. My technical proficiency now spans a wide range of technologies including Python & Java programming languages, Django, FastAPI, & Flask Backend Frameworks, and Angular, JavaScript, TypeScript, front end frameworks. I'm proficient AWS & Google Cloud Services and I've worked with NoSQL, SQL, and Hiearchical data bases.
